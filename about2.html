Meet Us

We are a team of youth programmers who aim to connect the people to healthcare services more efficiently. In this mordern era of digitalization, our goal is to provide people with a platform to enjoy the benefits of health related consultations without having to actually get out of their homes. we have also created with platform with the elderly people in mind, so that they may be able to get proper health consultations without facing the hassle of real life queues at the hospitals.
